## Convention

- This file is read by `/sprint-plan` at the start of each sprint and updated by `/capture-lessons` at the end.
- Completed items are **removed** (not commented out — history is in git).
- Items from review or red-team include their source: e.g. `Source: Sprint 03 — review, severity: major` or `Source: Sprint 05 — red-team, severity: medium`.
- Each item has an estimated complexity (S = a few hours, M = 1 day, L = several days).
